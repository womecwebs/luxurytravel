5. Visit a Private Elephant Sanctuary in Chiang Mai

In recent years, luxury travelers have become more conscious of the ethical implications of wildlife tourism—and nowhere is this more important than when it comes to Thailand's beloved elephants. Fortunately, Chiang Mai in Northern Thailand offers a new wave of private, responsible, and eco-luxurious elephant sanctuaries that deliver both meaningful connection and uncompromising comfort.

Skip the unethical tours and book a VIP sanctuary experience with Viator or Expedia:

Forget exploitative elephant rides and crowded tourist shows. Instead, picture yourself walking through serene bamboo forests with a gentle elephant by your side, bathing them in a jungle river, and enjoying panoramic views from your luxury eco-lodge.

  • One-on-one elephant bathing & feeding
  • Eco-lodges with mountain views
  • Guided jungle walks

Top Private Sanctuaries in Chiang Mai for VIP Travelers

Chiang Mai is the heart of ethical elephant tourism in Thailand, with several private sanctuaries offering premium experiences. Here are three of the best:

Elephant Nature Park - Private Tours

One of the most renowned elephant rescue centers in the world, Elephant Nature Park offers customizable private tours that include:

  • Personal guide and elephant caretaker
  • Private vegan lunch overlooking the valley
  • Exclusive access to observation areas
  • Opportunities to sponsor or adopt an elephant

Ideal for: Conservation-focused travelers and families

Baan Chang Elephant Park - Premium Experiences

Located in a peaceful mountain valley, Baan Chang focuses on small group interactions and offers premium packages including:

  • Full-day or overnight private programs
  • Feeding, bathing, and forest walks
  • Comfortable air-conditioned transport from your hotel
  • Private guide and flexible schedule

Ideal for: Couples or groups looking for a more intimate, semi-luxury experience without the crowds.
