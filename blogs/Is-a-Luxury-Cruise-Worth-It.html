Some travelers say YES (for the exclusivity & world-class service), while others feel luxury cruises are overpriced. The Cons of Booking a Luxury Cruise

❌ 1. High Price Tag ($5,000 - $100,000 per trip!)

❌ 2. Less Flexibility (Fixed Itineraries)

  • ⛔ Unlike private yachts, luxury cruises follow pre-set schedules
  • ⛔ Limited time in each destination (often 8-12 hours per stop)
