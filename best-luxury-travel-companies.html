Best Luxury Travel Companies

Compare trusted brands offering premium travel services — from private jets and first-class flights to luxury hotels, tours, and gear.

1. Expedia

Expedia - Premium Travel Services with VIP Hotel Access

Expedia is one of the most established travel platforms globally, offering everything from first-class flights to luxury resorts, cruises, and complete vacation packages. Its VIP Access program gives travelers access to select hotels offering perks like free upgrades, early check-in, or late checkout. Users can combine flights, hotels, and car rentals into one itinerary, often with discounted bundle rates. Expedia also has a rewards program that helps frequent travelers earn points and unlock member-only deals.

This platform is ideal for travelers looking for flexibility, global reach, and ease of comparison across multiple luxury hotel brands. Expedia supports over 2.9 million properties and thousands of airlines, making it a go-to platform for high-end and business travelers.

2. Viator

Viator - Curated Luxury Tours & Private Experiences

Viator, a TripAdvisor company, offers access to high-end travel experiences in over 2,500 destinations worldwide. From Tuscany to helicopter tours over the Grand Canyon or luxury yacht rentals in the Caribbean, Viator partners with local operators to deliver exclusive, customizable experiences.

It is especially well-suited for travelers looking for unique, skip-the-line, and premium activities with vetted providers. With 24/7 support, free cancellation on many bookings, and a strong user rating system, Viator is a trusted option for high-end excursions. Browse VIP Tours & Yacht Rentals

3. Villiers Jets

Villiers Jets - Private Jet Booking Platform with Global Access

Villiers Jets is a private aviation booking platform that gives travelers instant access to over 10,000 aircraft in 40,000+ locations worldwide. There are no upfront membership or subscription fees, and quotes are generated based on real-time availability and aircraft type. Users can charter flights on-demand, compare jet types, and receive 24/7 customer support.

Villiers works with certified operators and offers optional VIP concierge services including airport transfers and in-flight catering. It's a streamlined way to book a private jet for business, events, or luxury travel without a long-term commitment.

4. Kiwi.com

Kiwi.com - Smart Flight Bookings for Luxury & Flex Travelers

Kiwi.com uses a unique travel hacking system that combines flights from multiple carriers (including budget and premium) into one seamless itinerary. It also offers “Nomad” — an AI-powered multi-city travel planner — and hidden-city ticketing strategies to help travelers find lower prices on business and first-class flights.

While not exclusive to luxury, Kiwi.com appeals to smart travelers looking for premium seats at better prices, creative routing, and flexibility for one-way, multi-stop, or long-haul international trips. you can now book Smarter with Kiwi →

5. Hotellook

Hotellook - Instantly Compare Luxury Hotel Prices

Hotellook is a hotel comparison engine that aggregates offers from 100+ booking sites like Booking.com, Agoda, Hotels.com, and more. Travelers can filter by 5-star ratings, amenities, and location to instantly see the best rates across platforms.

This tool saves time and increases transparency — no need to bounce between hotel booking websites to find the lowest luxury room price.

6. KiwiTaxi

KiwiTaxi - Pre-Booked Chauffeur & Airport Transfer Services

KiwiTaxi allows travelers to schedule private transportation from airports, hotels, or cruise ports in over 100 countries. Vehicles range from business sedans to luxury SUVs, ideal for VIPs or families who want smooth, secure, and private ground transportation.

Prices are shown upfront with no hidden fees, and the platform offers multilingual support, waiting time, and door-to-door convenience. You can Choose Your Vehicle & Route and Get Private Chauffeur Pricing →

7. Amazon (Travel Gear)

Amazon - Travel Essentials for First-Class Comfort

Amazon is the leading global marketplace for luxury and practical travel gear. From premium luggage brands like TUMI and Samsonite to noise-canceling headphones, travel tech, and wellness kits, Amazon makes it easy for travelers to find essentials for high-comfort travel. The site features verified buyer reviews, Prime delivery options, and bundles tailored to frequent flyers and vacationers.

Whether you are heading on a luxury cruise, flying private, or staying in a five-star resort, Amazon offers reliable gear that elevates your travel experience without the retail markup of airport stores.
